Metaverse

Social platform where people interact through location-based AR

We encourage people to go outside and explore the real world. And along the way we will enrich the physical places with digital content creating the best of two worlds

Location-based

In the real world metaverse all digital content has its own place in the physical world. Once placed it will stay there waiting for people to see and interact with it

Dimensions

Each AR experience or creator project is a new dimension in the metaverse. The users will see all dimensions they chose at the same time without the need to switch between them. These dimensions can range from the size of a city to a small bedroom. While multiple dimensions may cover the same physical space, they do not interact with each other. They are called dimensions because they exist independently and separately from one another

Discover

A common place to discover, comment and share digital dimensions with your friends
